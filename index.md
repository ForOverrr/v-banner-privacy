# V_banner Privacy Policy

V_banner does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

- All data processed by the extension (course names, grades, student info) 
  is already visible to the logged-in student on bannerweb.ltu.edu and is 
  never sent anywhere.
- The only data saved locally is user preferences (UI toggle, pinned pages) 
  via chrome.storage.sync, which stays on your device.
- No analytics, no tracking, no third-party services.

Last updated: April 2026
